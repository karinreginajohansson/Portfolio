"Infographics"

Infographic and illustration assignment

Description

A common element for a designer can be to create information graphics, to try to visualize information, flows in a more comprehensible way than just using words and numbers. Especially in these times when we collect data we have to try to make users understand what it is used for.

How do you or your life look like in infographics form?

Choose what part or parts you want to highlight from your own life. There is a lot of information that can be visualized, but it is important to be able to limit yourself. Choose freely what you want to cover.

Solution

I wanted to choose something a little more unique than "how much coffee I drink" so I came to think of my absurd nail polish collection. I started collecting data on amount of nail polishes, how many colors, time I spend and spent and how much polish I've used... counted on the height... I then chose to compile this into an infographic and made all the illustrations in adobe illustrator.
