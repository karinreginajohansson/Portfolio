"Nox"

Brand design workshop project

Description

In this project we got four different AI generated cars and we got to choose one of them and create a visible brand for that car. The challenge was to come up with a name, logo and especially the feeling of that brand all the way through in both the digital and physical design. We also had to create a infotainmentsystem for this car and brand and all this in a couple of hours!

Solution

With the time preasure in mind, we sat down and got to work right away. Which car should we choose, what do we vision?

We saw this super chromed car reflecting the city lights by night in front of us and decided to go with a darker more mustic feeling of the night. The name "Nox" means night in latin so we chose that and then startet working on a pattern that we could see in the shape of the cars front. This pattern become our repetable thing in everything and we made the "x" in "Nox" from some shapes from this pattern. The Nox brand pattern.
