"Shuu"

Time preasured iterative groupwork

Description

In this project, the assignment was to create an online shop for the new shoe brand "Shuu". This brand would target mainly 40+ year olds but the rest was optional and the main point here was to come up with something quick (within an hour) to then go back and iterate.

Solution

For us, the vibe we got from the brands logo was definitely sneakers since we saw the shape of a sneaker in "Shuu". From a young audience, it's easy to think quite bold and cool when imagining a new sneakers shop but this task was mainly for midle-aged people. So what we decided to go for was a bit more mild design then we originally visioned and we decided to focused on nice, comfitable shoes and shoebrands instead, such as "Skechers" and "Ecco". We believe that with age, comfortability plays a bigger part then just "cool design", but we also wantet to create a space that could intreague a younger audience as well even though they're not the main customer.
