"Kristallklart"

Clarify the parties' thoughts on the housing market

Description

Before the election, we have many questions that concern us. How can we clarify the parties' thoughts on the housing market in an easily accessible way? The customer, "Hyresgästföreningen", wants us to test a quiz or questionnaire to clarify the opinions of the different parties. What does such a questionnaire or quiz look like? The idea is that your solution should work on mobile. If you come to the conclusion that a solution other than quiz or questionnaire is better, work with it, and argue why you did what you did.

"Hyresgästföreningen" believe they want a clickable solution on mobile. Questionnaires or quizzes that compile the views of the parties on the issue or something else that clarifies the position of the different parties: if you come up with something else that gives more information on the subject of housing policy in the different parties, please make suggestions for it. Think freely, empathetically and user-focused. The goal, the purpose, the driving force, pains and gains.

Solution

After an environmental analysis with interviews, we realized that many younger people do not understand the meaning of the questions and answers we receive from the various parties, i.e. the language. The app "Kristallklart" was created with focus on exactly that, to simplify the language so that a wider audience can be reached. Click on the crystal, the crystal translates and explains both the question and all different answers in simple language.
